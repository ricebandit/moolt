Hayk Manukyan

Hayk's career in animation began in 2000 at the age of 18. In his early years, he was fortunate enough to work alongside veteran artists and learn on the job. He has worked both as a 2D and 3D animator for such studios as Walt Disney, Warner Brothers, FOX, HBO, Comedy Central, Cartoon Network, Nickelodean and MTV. His work can be seen in such films as The Incredible Hulk, Alvin And The Chipmunks The Movie, and tv shows such as The Ricky Gervais Show and Teen Titans Go! Along with his professional work he has also created over 80 of his own personal shorts with a total of 8 million views world wide.

Brent Tanner

Brent has been working in production management for over 7 years on multiple flash and traditional animation projects. He has successfully managed projects for Cartoon Network, W!LDBRAIN Entertainment, and DHX Media, Los Angeles. He is currently the Transmedia Producer for a US Department of Education property at DHX Media, Los Angeles.

Shawn Toshikian

Shawn graduated from Otis College of Art and Design with a Bachelor of Arts in Illustration and Cogswell College with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer and Video Imaging. He started his career at a sculpting house doing production level proto-types for Disney, Warner Bros. and others. Since then he was the first Art Director for the #1 kid's website Neopets.com, an Animation Supervisor for Cartoon Network and Wildbrain/DHX Media, and Creative Director for Stand Alone Comics. He has worked on projects for Big Idea Entertainment, Cartoon Network, MTV, Mattel, Wildbrain/ DHX Media, Dreamworks, Warner Bros., and Marvel Animation. He is currently the Animation Supervisor on UMIGO.
